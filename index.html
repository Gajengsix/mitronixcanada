Our Services

MITRONIX CANADA manufactures high-performance fiber spray machines built for construction applications like insulation, fireproofing, and surface reinforcement. Our machines are durable, efficient, and easy to operate, helping construction crews save time, reduce waste, and improve application quality. We offer a range of fiber spray machines to meet the needs of various construction projects, from small residential jobs to large commercial developments. Our machines are designed to deliver consistent, reliable performance, making them an essential tool for any construction crew.

01. Fiber Spray Machine Manufacturing

MITRONIX CANADA specializes in designing and building fiber spray machines tailored to the specific needs of the industry. Whether you need a new machine built from scratch or a solution to meet unique performance requirements, our expert team ensures your equipment meets the highest standards of efficiency and durability.

02. Equipment Upgrades and Enhancements

At MITRONIX CANADA, we also offer machine upgrading services to help your existing equipment keep up with evolving industry needs. We provide enhancements that improve efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and integrate the latest technologies, ensuring your machines remain competitive and reliable.

03. Machine Repair and Maintenance

We provide comprehensive repair and maintenance services to keep your fiber spray machines running at peak performance. Our skilled technicians handle everything from minor repairs to major overhauls, ensuring minimal downtime and maximum productivity for your construction projects.
