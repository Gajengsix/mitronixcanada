Our Services

Techsoer is a software development company specializing in web-based application development services, providing tailored solutions to clients looking to improve their online presence and streamline their operations.

01.Website design and development

Techsoer is a premier web design and development company that specializes in creating beautiful, functional websites for businesses of all sizes. Our expert team of designers and developers work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and goals.

02. E-commerce development

Techsoer provides expert e-commerce development services for businesses looking to build a secure and user-friendly online store. From design to payment gateway integration, we create customized e-commerce solutions that drive sales and growth.

03. Mobile app development

At Techsoer, we specialize in creating custom mobile apps that are tailored to the specific needs of businesses. Our expert team of developers provides end-to-end services, including design, development, testing, and deployment, to help businesses succeed in the mobile marketplace.

About Us

We are Techsoer, a web-based application and website development company located in Jaffna, Sri Lanka. Our main focus is on building and maintaining web-based applications and websites. Additionally, we actively engage in research and development in the computer science field in Sri Lanka. Our company began as a startup in 2020 with a small group of friends, and we have successfully completed six projects thus far.
